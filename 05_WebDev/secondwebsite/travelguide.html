Destination Dimensions

  • Inverse Paris

This world is where all of the people are as nice as the Canadians in our world and not as stuck up. Here, many swapping of cultures happen and anything from the regular world had before will be reversal from what you are used to. There are no French Revolutions here, just Dance Dance Revolutions and this is all just one polite party.

  • Heaven

a vacation in paradise where you get served all the good things 24/7 and have endless amounts of joy and the only way to exit is to physically fall from a high mountain peak called Grace. Fortunately there is a portal lying on the bottom of it there. We left it there for fun because who would want to leave Heaven you monster.

  • Planes, Trains, and Frozenmobiles

this planet is just covered in ice cream and gelato in every flavor we could ever imagine and has a COOL transit system for you to tour through. Great for the sweet tooth, awful if you fall in trying to propose to your significant other, because after all, you can’t be a sweetheart and cold to someone at the same time.

Experience Dimensions

  • Re-Do

A dimension where you step into your alternate world self’s shoes in which you can live out what your life could have been if you made different choices by starting at the beginning of your life. Many paths will lead to many more and all the paths begin with the first choice. You may save and restart your lives at anytime but be careful, you may forget which one is your real life.

  • Super Smash Powers

Using Earth as the backdrop, you live our your life with superpowers that you can choose and develop throughout your stay here. The strength of your powers are directly related to the amount that you “donate” to us.

  • Refund Lab

a retail simulation mall where you choose to either shop till you drop or ethically sell things and legally obtain money. Comes with a stock market and Black Friday option to supercharge the madness.
