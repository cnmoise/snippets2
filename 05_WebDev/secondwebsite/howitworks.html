Cross-Dimensional Travel

The first step in establishing a destination to an alternate reality is by sending our team of expert glitchers (or dimensional travelers) to hack into a new dimension. Once we do a safety and quality evaluation of the reality in question our team of technicians “Worshipers of 11.00100100” calculate a portal at the desired reality.

How do we get you there?

It’s quite simple, much like the now defunct airline industry you book a ticket through our website (that’s where you are now) and select a dimension you would like to travel to and a portal “departure” time which suits your needs. Then you come to one of our compounds at the scheduled time and place and you’re on your way!
