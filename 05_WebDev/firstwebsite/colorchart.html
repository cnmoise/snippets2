The color I found most interesting is purple. Given that it is a combination of blue and red it is the most flexible and restful color of the colors I researched. Blue and purple are analogous colors so they will work best together in creating a harmonious page. The added benefit of having access both blue and purple is that blue works well with khaki and other browns. Purple also has a certain ambiguity and grandeur which captivates my imagination.
