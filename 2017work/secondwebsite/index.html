How it started

A daring man named Ralph Henderson breaks into Area 51 on the 27th of May 1979 and aquires a piece of alien technology being kept secret by the CIA. Not only did Henderson get away with it but he brought this technology to mass market by founding Elsewhere Inc. He is now a dimensionally recognized business-man.

After acquiring a few pieces of alien technology from Ralph Henderson, Elseworld Inc. started the process of modifying them to fit the needs of every living organism in the universe. This was no easy task. Our very first discovery was our most painful one. The portal generator was genetically coded to be used specifically for the Alien Grays. One of our more reckless early members decided to enter the prototype portal without hesitation. He was instantly vaporized.

It took almost a decade to analyze the alien machinery in secrecy. Since the alien language was totally unknown, we sent out a signal through the portal for help. What we received blew our minds. The signals we received ranged from threats of a cease and desist from the Galactic Federation to proper help from the Alien Grays. The Alien Grays informed us that the Gray in the Roswell Incident was a high-profile fugitive and thanked us for his capture. They rewarded us with reality-bending information. The Grays explained that the portal generator was a “simple” piece of technology. Simply put, the portal generator is utilized through Quantum Entanglement that incorporates Wormholes. The bidirectional travel is applied with Quantum Tunneling.
